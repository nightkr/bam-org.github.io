BAM is an add-on for the game Blockland, providing a variety of useful in-game features as well as improvements and replacements for RTB features. It's an open initiative, and community feedback is greatly appreciated.

Features Subject to change.

In-game chat

Talk to your friends or just about anybody playing Blockland, no matter what server you are on.

Add-on gallery and management

Browse through a moderated gallery of add-ons conforming to quality standards with user reviews, recommendations and more. Easily download and manage add-ons directly from the game.

Server preferences

Customize a wide variety of built-in and add-on provided settings of your server, all without restarting it or having to leave the game.

And much more to come!

BAM is under ongoing development, and not even this list is complete. Look forward to many new, useful features.

Colorset manager

Use custom colorsets on your server with a simple, user-friendly list and previews. No need to mess with server files anymore.

RTB add-on compatibility

Server preferences and other BAM features support legacy RTB add-ons. Their additions will seamlessly be integrated into the new system.

Open source development

With an open source add-on, open source interface, and an open source review process, you can be sure that you are getting only the most secure, stable add-ons, delivered through only the most secure, stable system.
